Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

To apply for a death certificate, log in to your account, navigate to the 'Apply for a Death Certificate' section, and follow the instructions. You will need to provide necessary details such as the deceased’s information and submit the required documents.

You can check the status of your PAN card application by logging into your account and visiting the 'Check PAN Card Status' section. Enter your application reference number to view the current status.

To update your driving license information, log in to your account, go to the 'Update Driving License Information' section, and follow the instructions. You'll need to provide the updated information and any necessary documents.

To apply for a ration card, log in to your account and navigate to the 'Apply for Ration Card' section. Fill out the application form with the required details and upload the necessary documents. Submit the form for processing.

For support, you can contact our helpdesk via the 'Contact Us' page on our website. You can reach out to us through email or phone, and our support team will assist you with any issues or questions you may have.
